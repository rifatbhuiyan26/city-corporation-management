# 🏙️ City Corporation Management System

A structured **Object-Oriented Programming** project designed to manage the internal hierarchy and operations of a city corporation.  
This system focuses on managing key roles such as **Mayor, Counsellor, Secretary, and Staff/Workers** along with different service-related categories like **food, place, and staff sections**.

---

## 📌 Project Overview

The **City Corporation Management System** is designed to organize and streamline the management structure of a city corporation.  
It represents how different administrative roles are connected and how they handle different responsibilities inside the organization.

The system mainly focuses on:

- Managing administrative roles
- Maintaining hierarchical relationships
- Organizing staff and service categories
- Structuring city corporation data clearly and efficiently

This project is especially useful for understanding **Object-Oriented Programming concepts**, **entity relationships**, and **system design**.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

- To build a structured city corporation management system
- To represent real-world administrative hierarchy in software form
- To manage roles like Mayor, Counsellor, Secretary, and Staff
- To improve data organization and record handling
- To practice OOP concepts using a meaningful real-life project

---

## 🧩 Main Entities in the System

### 1. Admin
The admin controls and manages the system access.  
It includes login-related attributes such as:

- ID
- UserName
- Password

### 2. Mayor
The mayor is one of the main authorities in the city corporation.  
The mayor leads and supervises important operational roles.

### 3. Counsellor
Counsellors work under the mayor and handle different responsibilities.  
They also assist in connecting different parts of the management workflow.

### 4. Secretary
The secretary works closely with the counsellor and manages several operational choices and internal support tasks.

### 5. Staff
The staff section includes different working categories such as:

- Electronics
- Cloth
- Cockeries
- Toys

### 6. Food
The system also includes food-related categories such as:

- Thai
- Chinese
- Bangladeshi
- Indian

### 7. Place
The place section contains different location choices such as:

- Army Stadium
- Ramna
- ICCB
- TSC

---

## 🔗 Relationship Summary

The system follows a connected hierarchy where different entities interact with one another.

Some important relationships include:

- Admin manages login and system control
- Mayor leads counsellors
- Counsellor handles and assists secretary-related activities
- Secretary works with staff and makes choices
- Place and food selections are connected to organizational decisions

This relational structure helps represent a full management workflow in a visual and logical way.
